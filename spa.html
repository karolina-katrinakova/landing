The recovery ritual

Heat, cold, repeat

Our favourite post-class ritual: 20 minutes in the infrared sauna, followed by a 2-minute cold plunge, then 10 minutes of rest in the relaxation room with a herbal tea. Three rounds of this and your nervous system resets completely. It is not a luxury. It is maintenance.

  • Infrared sauna heats the body directly, not the air
  • Cold plunge at 4°C for circulation and recovery
  • Complimentary herbal tea with every session
  • Robes and slippers provided
Train + recover

The full loop

Most people pair their class with a spa session. Reformer pilates or yoga, then straight into the sauna and cold plunge. It all happens in the same building, on the same visit.

Recovery starts here

From the studio to the spa

Finish your class, grab a towel, and walk straight into the recovery wing. The sauna is pre-heated. The plunge pool is at 4 degrees. Robes, slippers, and herbal tea are waiting. Most members add 45 minutes of spa time to every visit. It's become part of the ritual.

  • Steps from the reformer and yoga studios
  • Robes and slippers included
  • Complimentary herbal tea after every session
  • Monthly spa pass available for unlimited access
