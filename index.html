Our space

Designed to make you stay

Warm materials, natural light, and thoughtful details in every corner. Bloom feels less like a studio and more like a place you actually want to spend time in.

Architecture & interiors

Every detail is intentional

Concrete floors, linen curtains, arched mirrors, and olive trees. We worked with a Prague-based architecture studio to create a space that feels calm and warm from the moment you walk in. The reformer studio has floor-to-ceiling windows. The yoga room is lit by candles. The matcha bar is where everyone gathers after class.

  • Prague 2, Vinohrady neighbourhood
  • 850 m² across two floors
  • Changing rooms with rainfall showers
  • Rooftop terrace for summer events
  • Free filtered water stations throughout
The ritual

Pilates, matcha, repeat

Bloom is built around a daily loop: move, recover, refuel. Grab your matcha, warm up on the reformer, push through class, then sit down for brunch with friends. It all happens here.

More than a studio

Where fitness meets lifestyle

Every class comes with a complimentary matcha or protein shake. The matcha bar is steps from the studio, so you're sipping before the sweat even dries. After class, stay for an acai bowl, book a massage, or just sit in the sun on the terrace. Most people plan to stay an hour. They stay three.

Why Bloom

Everything in one place

Most studios do one thing. We built a place where your workout, your recovery, and your post-class ritual all happen under the same roof.

Train smarter

Reformer pilates and yoga in the same studio. Mix and match based on how your body feels, not which gym you signed up for.

Recover after every session

Infrared sauna, cold plunge, and massage are steps away from the studio. Recovery is not a separate trip — it is part of the visit.

Matcha on us

Every class comes with a complimentary matcha. Or stay longer — the kitchen serves acai bowls, protein smoothies, and proper food.

Community, not a membership card

Run club is free for everyone. Workshops are open to non-members. Bloom is a place where people actually want to hang out.

No contracts, ever

Month-to-month memberships, class packs, or single drop-ins. Cancel anytime. Your first class is always free.

Designed for your morning

Rainfall showers, hair dryers, toiletries, and towels. Come before work, leave ready for the day. No gym bag required.
