Reformer Pilates

Full-body, low-impact sessions that build strength and control without the strain. Small groups, expert coaching, and the calm of our reformer studio.

50 & 75 minute sessions

Full-body, low-impact, high results

Train in small groups of up to eight so every session feels personal. We welcome all levels, from first-timers to seasoned movers. Join us on Sundays for a 75-minute deep reformer class that goes further: slower pacing, full-body focus, and space to refine your practice.

  • Small groups, max 8
  • Balanced Body machines
  • All levels welcome
  • Posture, core strength, and flexibility
  • Sunday 10:00 75-min deep reformer
  • Complimentary matcha after class

What to expect

Your first class

No experience needed

Arrive 10 minutes early so we can set up your reformer and walk you through the basics. Wear comfortable clothes that let you move. We provide grip socks, towels, and a mat. Your instructor will offer modifications throughout so the class meets you exactly where you are. Afterwards, head to the matcha bar — your first drink is on us.

  • Arrive 10 min early for your first session
  • Grip socks and towels provided
  • Modifications for every exercise
  • Post-class matcha included

Our studios

Two rooms, one philosophy

Bloom has two reformer studios, each with its own character. The main studio has floor-to-ceiling arched mirrors and natural light. The second is moodier — sculptural mirrors, dim lighting, deeper focus.

The main studio

Light, airy, and open

Our primary reformer room seats eight machines beneath curved ceilings and backlit arches. The room is flooded with natural light from full-height windows, and the floor is polished concrete. Plants and olive trees soften the space. This is where most classes happen, from 7:00 morning sessions to evening wind-downs.

More than a workout

The Bloom reformer experience

It's not just about the class. It's showing up early with your matcha, warming up on the reformer, pushing through 50 focused minutes, then sitting in the cafe with your friends after. That's the Bloom loop.

Reformer + matcha culture

Where fitness meets lifestyle

Every class comes with a complimentary matcha or protein shake from our in-house bar. Arrive early, grab your drink, warm up on your machine. After class, linger at the cafe, book your next session, or head straight to the spa for recovery. It's all in one building.

  • Complimentary matcha or shake after every class
  • In-studio grip socks and towels
  • Spa access for members
  • Post-class brunch menu available
