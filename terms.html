2. Memberships

All memberships are month-to-month with no lock-in contract. You may cancel or pause your membership at any time; changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Membership fees are non-refundable for the current billing period.

3. Class bookings & cancellations

Classes can be booked through our website or at reception. We require at least 12 hours' notice for cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows may result in the class being deducted from your pack or a late cancellation fee of €10 for drop-in clients.

4. Class packs

Class packs are valid for 3 months from the date of purchase. Unused classes after the expiry period are non-refundable and non-transferable. Class packs are for personal use only.

5. Health & safety

You are responsible for disclosing any medical conditions, injuries, or pregnancy to your instructor before class. Bloom reserves the right to refuse participation if we believe it poses a health risk. All participants exercise at their own risk.

6. Studio etiquette

  • Late arrivals may not be admitted to class
  • Clean equipment after use
  • Mobile phones on silent during classes
  • Respect shared spaces and other members

7. Spa & recovery

Spa facilities are available during studio opening hours. Follow all posted guidelines for sauna, cold plunge, and steam room use. Maximum sauna session: 60 minutes. Cold plunge should not exceed 5 minutes per session. Bloom is not liable for any adverse reactions to heat or cold therapy.
