Our story

About Bloom

We are a wellness studio in Prague's Vinohrady — a calm, considered home for pilates, yoga, recovery, and the ritual of a very good matcha afterwards. This is how we came to be, and what we believe in.

Our story

One roof, one intention

Vinohrady, Prague 2

Built from a simple idea

Bloom began when our founder kept leaving one place to train, another to recover, and somewhere else entirely just to eat something that felt good. Vinohrady deserved a studio that did not ask you to run across the city to feel whole.

The vision was straightforward: movement, recovery, and nourishment under one roof — not as a trend, but as a rhythm you could actually keep. We wanted a room that felt like morning light in the Mediterranean, instructors who remember your name, and a bar where conversations linger as long as the foam on your matcha.

What started as a sketch on paper became walls of warm plaster, linen, and wood — a space honest enough for your hardest week and gentle enough for your quietest Sunday.

What we stand for

Our values

Small choices, repeated daily, shape how a studio feels.

Movement for everyone

First class or five hundredth, your body is welcome here. We teach progress, not performance — and we mean it.

Quiet over loud

No shouting playlists, no chaos in the corridors. We protect calm so you can hear yourself think — and breathe.

Quality over quantity

Fewer classes on the schedule, more attention in each one. Equipment, ingredients, and people — we invest where it touches you.

Community, not competition

The person on the reformer next to you is not your rival. We grow stronger when we root for each other — in the studio and at the bar.

The space

Light, linen, and intention

Mediterranean calm in Prague

Designed to slow you down

We drew inspiration from coastal architecture — soft arches, breathable materials, and walls that catch the sun instead of fighting it. Natural light pours into the training rooms; warm wood and stone underfoot ground you the moment you step inside.

Linen curtains soften sound, plants blur the edge between indoors and out, and every corner is meant to feel lived-in, not staged. The space asks nothing of you except to arrive as you are.

Come see for yourself

Take a class on us and feel whether Bloom fits your life. No pitch — just the space, the people, and the light.
