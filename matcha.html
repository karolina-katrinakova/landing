Matcha Bar & Kitchen

Ceremonial-grade matcha whisked to order. Post-workout brunch made from scratch. The part of the workout you actually look forward to.

Sourced with care

Matcha that actually tastes like matcha

We source our ceremonial-grade matcha directly from a single farm in Uji, Kyoto — the same region that's supplied Japan's tea ceremonies for over 800 years. The powder is stone-ground, air-shipped, and kept refrigerated until it reaches your cup.

Every cup is whisked to order by hand. No premixes, no powdered concentrate, no green-coloured sugar water. Hot, iced, or layered with oat milk — you'll taste the difference from the first sip. That vivid, almost electric green? That's what real matcha looks like.

  • Single-origin from Uji, Kyoto
  • Stone-ground and air-shipped fresh
  • Whisked to order, never premixed
  • Oat, almond, and coconut milk options

The craft

Every cup is a small ceremony

Bamboo chasen, handmade chawan, sifted powder, 80-degree water. Our baristas don't just make matcha — they prepare it. The whole process takes ninety seconds and it's worth watching every time.

We use the same tools and the same method that tea masters in Kyoto have used for centuries. The only difference is we're doing it in Vinohrady, and you can drink it on a reformer. This is the detail that separates a matcha bar from a green latte counter.

Sip slow. Move well. Come back.

The Bloom ritual

More than a bar

The gathering place

The matcha bar is the social heart of Bloom. It's where you debrief after a tough reformer class, where run club members swap race stories, and where strangers become friends over a shared acai bowl. Concrete countertops, live-edge shelves lined with handmade ceramics, and the quiet hum of the espresso machine. The communal table seats twelve. The morning light through the arched windows is worth the visit alone.

  • Communal table for post-class socialising
  • Oat, almond, and coconut milk options
  • All food made fresh in-house daily
  • Vegan, gluten-free, and dairy-free options
  • 10% off for All Access members

It's a lifestyle

Pilates, matcha, repeat

At Bloom, the matcha bar isn't an afterthought — it's the other half of the ritual. Grab your iced matcha before class, set it on the reformer, move for 50 minutes, and then come back for brunch. That's the loop.
