Bloom Run Club

Four runs a week, all levels welcome. Free to join, no membership required.

Four runs per week

Your city is the course

Bloom Run Club is more than a jog around the block. We run four times a week with different formats, distances, and vibes. Whether you want to push your pace, explore new routes, or just get moving with good people, there is a run for you. Every session ends back at Bloom with a complimentary matcha or smoothie.

  • Monday 17:30 — Tempo run (coached pacing through Riegrovy Sady)
  • Wednesday 17:30 — Intervals (speed work, hill repeats)
  • Thursday 18:00 — Long run (8-15K, rotating city routes)
  • Saturday 11:30 — Social run (all paces, post-run matcha)
  • Monthly race prep programme for 10K and half marathon
  • Complimentary matcha or smoothie after every run
  • Free to join, no membership required

The community

Run together, recover together

What makes Bloom Run Club different from running alone is what happens after. Everyone comes back to the studio, stretches out on the terrace, and shares a matcha or smoothie together. Friendships form quickly when you have shared six kilometres of hill repeats. We also run a monthly race prep programme for anyone training for a 10K or half marathon.

  • All paces welcome, nobody left behind
  • Coached pace groups for structured training
  • Monthly race prep for 10K and half marathon
  • Post-run stretch and mobility on the terrace
  • Matcha or smoothie after every single run

Ready to run?

Just show up. No sign-up needed. We meet at Bloom and run from there.
