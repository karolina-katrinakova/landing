Signature workshop

The art of matcha

Our most popular workshop. Learn the full Japanese matcha ceremony from sifting to whisking. You will taste ceremonial-grade matcha from different regions, learn to read quality by colour and texture, and walk away knowing how to make the perfect bowl at home. Includes a matcha tasting flight and your own bamboo chasen to keep.

  • 90-minute hands-on session, max 12 people
  • Matcha swatch comparison and tasting flight
  • Learn to use a chasen, chashaku, and chawan
  • Take home your own bamboo whisk
  • Runs every second Saturday at 11:00
  • €65 per person (members save 10%)
