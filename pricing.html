Questions

Frequently asked

Is my first class really free?

Yes. Your very first class at Bloom is completely free, no strings attached. Book any reformer pilates or yoga session, show up, and try it. We will even give you a matcha afterwards.

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

Absolutely. All memberships are month-to-month with no lock-in contract. Cancel or pause anytime via your account or at reception. Your membership stays active until the end of your current billing cycle.

What is the difference between class packs and memberships?

Memberships renew monthly and offer the best per-class value plus extras like spa access and priority booking. Class packs are a one-time purchase with no recurring charge — perfect if you prefer flexibility or come less regularly. Packs are valid for 3 months.

Can I switch between membership plans?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Just let us know at reception or send us an email.

Do class packs include spa access?

The 10 and 20 class packs include complimentary spa sessions (1 and 2 respectively). Beyond that, spa treatments can be booked separately as single sessions. Only the All Access membership includes unlimited spa access.

Is the run club really free?

Yes. Bloom Run Club is free for everyone — members and non-members. Just show up at the meeting point and run with us. No registration, no fee. Matcha or smoothie after every run is on us.

Do you offer student or couple discounts?

We offer a 15% discount on all memberships for students with a valid ISIC card. Couples signing up together get 10% off each membership. Both discounts apply to the monthly rate and can be combined with the free first class.

How do guest passes work?

Unlimited and All Access members receive guest passes each month (1 and 2 respectively). A guest pass allows a friend to attend any single class for free. Just add their name when you book. Guests also get a complimentary matcha.

Ready to start?

Your first class is free. No credit card needed. Pick a time and we will see you there.
