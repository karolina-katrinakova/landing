Yoga

Slow down, breathe, reconnect. Flow, yin, and restorative sessions in a warm, candlelit studio.

60 & 90 minute sessions

Slow down, breathe, reconnect

Our yoga sessions are led by certified instructors in a warm, candlelit studio. We offer flow yoga, yin yoga, and restorative sessions throughout the week. Each class ends with a 10-minute guided meditation to help you carry the calm into the rest of your day. On Saturdays we host a 90-minute extended session combining slow flow with deep restorative holds and breathwork.

  • Flow, yin, and restorative styles
  • Warm, candlelit studio space
  • All mats and props provided
  • 10-minute guided meditation at the end
  • Saturday 8:00 — 90-min slow flow & restore
  • Beginner-friendly, no experience needed
Our philosophy

Movement as meditation

We do not believe yoga is about touching your toes. It is about learning to breathe when things are difficult, finding steadiness when everything feels unsteady, and carrying that calm into the rest of your life.

Three styles, one intention

Find the practice that fits you

Our flow yoga classes build heat and rhythm through movement. Yin yoga is slower and deeper, holding poses for three to five minutes to release the connective tissue. Restorative sessions use bolsters and blankets to let your body completely let go. Every class ends with savasana and a 10-minute guided meditation.

  • Flow yoga — dynamic, breath-led movement
  • Yin yoga — long holds, deep release
  • Restorative — fully supported, deeply restful
  • Heated room option for flow classes (30°C)
The studio

A space designed for stillness

Concrete floors, arched mirrors, linen curtains, and warm indirect lighting. Our yoga room was designed with an architecture studio to create a space that slows you down the moment you walk in.

Thoughtful design

Warm materials, calm lighting

The yoga studio features hand-troweled plaster walls, wooden shelving for our equipment, and backlit arch mirrors that cast a soft glow throughout the space. Temperature is controlled to stay at a comfortable 24°C for regular classes, with a heated option at 30°C for flow classes.

  • Hand-troweled plaster walls
  • Backlit arched mirrors
  • Premium mats, bolsters, and blocks provided
  • Heated room option (30°C)
  • Natural essential oil diffusers
